## What they emphasize

- **Complex state** — editors, undo/redo, tools, selection.
- **Canvas / graphics** — rendering, transforms, performance.
- **JS depth** — closures, prototypes, event handling.
- **Performance** for heavy interactive UIs.

## Prep plan (2 weeks)

1. Build a canvas-based tool (draw/select/transform).
2. Implement undo/redo with the [command pattern](../18-design-patterns/).
3. Review [JS internals](../03-javascript/) deeply.
4. Study a collaborative-editor [design](../15-system-design/design-google-docs.md).
