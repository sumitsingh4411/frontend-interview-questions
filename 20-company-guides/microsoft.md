## What they emphasize

- **Accessibility** — WCAG, ARIA, keyboard. Frequently and seriously scored.
- **TypeScript** — typing components and APIs well.
- **Fundamentals** — DOM, JS, DSA balance.
- **Collaboration & growth mindset** in behavioral.

## Prep plan (2 weeks)

1. Go deep on [accessibility](../11-accessibility/) (ARIA APG patterns).
2. Sharpen [TypeScript](../04-typescript/).
3. Balance DSA with 3–4 accessible components.
4. Prepare growth-mindset behavioral stories.
