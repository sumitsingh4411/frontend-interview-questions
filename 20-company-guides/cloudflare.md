## What they emphasize

- **Networking** — HTTP/2-3, caching, CDN, TLS. Expect real depth here.
- **Performance** — edge rendering, Core Web Vitals.
- **Data dashboards** — analytics, real-time metrics, large tables.
- **Edge runtime** (Workers) awareness is a plus.
