## What they emphasize

- **Component craft** — clean API, states handled, pixel-accurate, reusable.
- **Accessibility** — keyboard, ARIA, focus. Frequently scored.
- **Design systems** — you'll discuss building reusable components at scale.
- **Data-driven product sense.**

## Prep plan (2 weeks)

1. Build 5 components to a **high polish bar** (all states + a11y + tests).
2. Study [accessibility](../11-accessibility/) patterns (dialog, combobox, focus).
3. Practice design-system / component-library architecture.
4. Prepare core-values behavioral stories.
