## What they emphasize

- **Feed architecture** — infinite scroll, virtualization, caching, real-time.
- **Accessibility & i18n** — global product, many languages, strong a11y bar.
- **Performance** — feed rendering, image loading, CLS.
- **Practical React/JS.**
